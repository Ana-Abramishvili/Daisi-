DAISI

Georgian - Café-Restaurant

Welcome to DAISI, where the charm of Georgian tradition meets contemporary flair. Nestled in the heart of the city, DAISI is more than just a place to dine—it's an experience that invites you to savor the rich, authentic flavors of Georgia. Our carefully curated menu celebrates the soulful dishes passed down through generations, while embracing modern twists to tantalize your taste buds. Whether you're enjoying a leisurely brunch, a cozy coffee break, or an intimate dinner, DAISI offers a warm and inviting atmosphere, where every meal feels like a celebration of life. Come, let us share with you the essence of Georgian hospitality, where every guest is treated like family, and every dish tells a story.
